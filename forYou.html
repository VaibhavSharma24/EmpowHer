Government Schemes for Women

  • Pradhan Mantri Matru Vandana Yojana - A maternity benefit programme that provides financial assistance of Rs. 5,000 in three instalments to pregnant and lactating women for their first living child.
  • Ujjwala Scheme - A scheme for providing free LPG connections to women of BPL households to promote clean cooking fuel and reduce indoor air pollution caused by traditional cooking fuels.
  • Beti Bachao Beti Padhao - A campaign aimed at promoting gender equality and the education of girls in India.
  • National Scholarship for Higher Education for ST Students - A scholarship for ST students pursuing professional courses at the graduate and post-graduate level to encourage them to pursue higher education and increase their representation in various professional fields.
  • Pradhan Mantri Scheme for Empowerment of Women - A scheme aimed at empowering women by providing opportunities for education, employment, and skill development.
